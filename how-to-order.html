Platform overview

What is LoveGoBuy?

LoveGoBuy is a cross-border e-commerce platform that simplifies international shopping on Chinese marketplaces. It handles purchasing assistance, quality inspection, warehousing, and worldwide shipping, removing every barrier between you and the product: language, payment, and logistics.

LoveGoBuy is the cheapest agent on the market and one of the fastest, with delivery averaging around 10 days to your door.

Step by step

How to use LoveGoBuy

1

Create your account

Sign up via the link on the homepage, order now and unlock a 500€ bonus, exclusive coupons, and 1 free item.

You can create secondary accounts to claim new coupons once the previous ones are used up.
2

Find your pieces

Browse the spreadsheet on the homepage (updated regularly) or join the Archive Fashion Discord for drops, finds, and community picks.

3

Place your order

Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout. LoveGoBuy handles purchasing on your behalf if needed. Items typically arrive at the warehouse within 5 days.

4

Quality check & shipping

Once your items reach the warehouse, LoveGoBuy performs a full quality inspection (QC) and sends you photos. After approval, you select a shipping line and pay the fees.

DHL and UPS are both reliable. UPS is the preferred choice for most orders.
5

Track your package

Everything is managed directly on the LoveGoBuy website. Tracking updates automatically from warehouse departure to your front door. Expect delivery within ~10 days.
