Shipping is charged by weight, around 9€/kg. A single jersey weighs around 200 to 250g, so you need to order 4 to 5 jerseys minimum to reach 1kg and make the shipping worthwhile.

A standard jersey takes 2 to 5 days to reach the Lovegobuy warehouse. With customization (name, number or patch), add 1 to 2 weeks at the warehouse. From the warehouse to your home, expect another 1 to 2 weeks. Total time varies by seller and shipping method chosen.
